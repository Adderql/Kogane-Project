# Product

## Register

product

## Users

You, first — then a public release. Someone who wants a capable AI companion living on their desktop, not in a browser tab. They're comfortable with hotkeys and expect the tool to stay out of their way until summoned. When it appears, it feels personal, not generic.

## Product Purpose

Kogane is a floating AI companion that lives on the desktop as a character. Summoned by hotkey, it handles quick questions, screen reading, history, and multi-modal tasks without breaking the user's flow. Success means it feels less like a tool and more like something that belongs to the user specifically.

## Brand Personality

Intimate, precise, alive. The face is not decoration — it IS the interface. Kogane has presence: it breathes, bounces, poof-appears. Character-driven rather than chrome-driven. Warmth without being playful; personality without being loud.

## Anti-references

- OS system widgets (Cortana, old Clippy): chunky, generic, feels like it came with the computer
- SaaS dashboards: cards everywhere, sidebar nav, productivity-tool aesthetic
- ChatGPT/Claude web: centered chat layout, white background, institutional text bubbles

## Design Principles

1. Character before chrome — Kogane IS the interface. The face, the motion, and the presence define the product; the surrounding UI is just the stage it performs on.
2. Precision over explanation — State is shown, not described. Indicators, density, and motion convey information; labels are used only when nothing else works.
3. Alive, not animated — Motion is behavior. Kogane breathes, bounces on response, poof-appears. Every animation should feel like the character acting, not like a CSS transition.
4. Compact and present — The panel appears near the user's focus, stays as small as possible, and disappears completely when dismissed. Never permanent chrome.
5. Yours — Designed to feel owned and specific, never generic SaaS. The user should feel like they have something others don't.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keyboard-first: every function reachable via hotkey. No WCAG compliance target yet, but the dark palette must maintain sufficient contrast for primary text and interactive states.
